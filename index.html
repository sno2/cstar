The polar angle is the distance in degrees from the horizon to Polaris, the North Star. Find the polar angle by locating the big dipper and following the two stars at the end of the cup to the North Star.

The solar noon is the time in your timezone that the Sun is directly above your location. It would be best to look this up for your location before setting out on your offline travels. However, it can be obtained anywhere by placing a stick (or similar object) into the ground. Now, the time at which the stick does not have a shadow is the solar noon.
